I am a Ph.D. student in Mathematics at South China University of Technology. My research focuses on integrable systems and nonlinear wave equations, especially orbital stability, finite-gap solutions, and spectral methods for nonlinear Schrödinger-type equations and the AKNS hierarchy.

I am particularly interested in the connection between Lax spectra, squared eigenfunctions, Lyapunov functionals, and stability problems for solitons and periodic algebro-geometric solutions.
