My research lies at the intersection of integrable systems, nonlinear dispersive equations, and spectral stability theory.

Current topics include:

- **Orbital stability of solitons and multi-solitons.** I study Lyapunov functionals, negative directions of linearized operators, and the role of Lax spectra in stability problems.
- **Periodic finite-gap solutions of focusing NLS.** I am interested in algebro-geometric solutions, Abelian differentials, theta-function formulae, and spectral criteria for stability.
- **Squared eigenfunctions and completeness.** I study how squared eigenfunctions and adjoint squared eigenfunctions represent perturbations of integrable PDEs and how this can be used in linearized dynamics.
- **AKNS hierarchy and conservation laws.** I am interested in the relation between quasi-momentum, higher-order conserved quantities, and variational structures for integrable flows.

The long-term goal is to develop a more structural stability theory for nonlinear waves in integrable systems, especially beyond the soliton case and in periodic finite-gap backgrounds.
